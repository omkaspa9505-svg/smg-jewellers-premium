## Part 1: Setting up your tracking "Command Center"
Before we spend a single Rupee on ads, we need to know who is visiting and what they are doing. Follow these steps:

### 1. Google Tag Manager (GTM)
Think of GTM as the "Bridge" between your code and your tools.
- **Task:** Go to [tagmanager.google.com](https://tagmanager.google.com/) and create a "Container" for `Web`.
- **Outcome:** You will get an ID like `GTM-XXXXXXX`. Send that to me.

### 2. GA4 (Google Analytics)
This tells you *where* your traffic comes from (Google, Instagram, WhatsApp).
- **Task:** Go to [analytics.google.com](https://analytics.google.com/) and create a new Property.
- **Outcome:** You will get a Measurement ID like `G-XXXXXXXX`. (Save this, you don't need to give it to me; we store it in GTM later).

### 3. Hotjar (Behavior Tracking)
This shows you **Heatmaps** and **Video Recordings** of people on your site. You will literally see where they click and why they leave.
- **Task:** Go to [hotjar.com](https://www.hotjar.com/) and add your site URL.
- **Outcome:** You will get a "Site ID." (Save this).
