# Meta Ads "Click-To-WhatsApp" Crash Course
**For SMG Jewellers - Akshaya Tritiya Campaign**

If you don't know anything about Meta advertising, do not worry. This is a highly targeted, zero-BS guide on how to launch the exact "Direct-to-WhatsApp" campaign you need using your uncle's poster. 

> [!WARNING]
> **The Golden Rule:** NEVER use the "Boost Post" button on Instagram or Facebook. It is a waste of money. Always use the **Meta Ads Manager** (`business.facebook.com`) on your computer.

---

## Phase 1: The Setup (Do this once)
1. **Get WhatsApp Business:** You must have the [WhatsApp Business App](https://business.whatsapp.com/) (not the regular WhatsApp) installed and registered with the store's number (e.g., 8008666199).
2. **Link Accounts:** Go to your SMG Jewellers Facebook Page Settings -> Linked Accounts, and connect both your Instagram account and your WhatsApp Business number.
3. **Go to Ads Manager:** Log into [Ads Manager](https://adsmanager.facebook.com/).

---

## Phase 2: Creating the Campaign

### 1. The Objective
Click the green **"Create"** button. 
*   **Campaign Objective:** Choose **"Engagement"**. *(Do not choose Traffic or Brand Awareness).*
*   Click **Continue**.

### 2. The Ad Set (Targeting details)
This is where you tell Meta *who* to show the ad to.
*   **Conversion Location:** Select "Messaging Apps".
*   **Ad Type:** Click to message.
*   **Messaging Apps:** Check *only* **WhatsApp**. (Uncheck Messenger and Instagram Direct).
*   **Budget:** Set your daily budget (e.g., ₹500 - ₹1,000/day to start). Set the end date to May 16th.
*   **Audience Location:** DO NOT target all of Hyderabad. Drop a pin on **Chanda Nagar** and set the radius to **5km - 10km max**. Local retail thrives on proximity. 
*   **Age:** 25 - 60. (People who actually have money to buy 1KG of silver).
*   **Detailed Targeting:** Keep it simple. Add: `Jewellery`, `Silver`, `Investment`, `Tanishq` (to target competitor audiences), `Gold`.

### 3. The Ad (What they see)
*   **Ad Setup:** Single image or video.
*   **Media:** Upload the exact poster your uncle sent you.
*   **Primary Text (The Caption):** Keep it punchy. 
    > *"Exclusive Akshaya Tritiya Offers at SMG Jewellers, Chanda Nagar! ✨ 
    > 
    > 🔹 Flat ₹10,000 OFF on 1KG Silver
    > 🔹 50% OFF Making on Antique Silver
    > 🔹 0% Extra Charges on 24KT Gold Bars
    >
    > Tap the WhatsApp button below to lock in your live market price today!"*
*   **Headline:** "Chat on WhatsApp to Lock Offer"
*   **Call to Action:** Send WhatsApp Message.

### 4. Message Template (Crucial Step)
Meta will let you create an automated greeting when they click into WhatsApp.
*   Set the **Customer Action** to "Send a pre-filled message".
*   **Pre-filled message:** *"Hi SMG Jewellers, I'm interested in the Akshaya Tritiya Silver/Gold offers. Can I lock in the discount?"*

---

## Phase 3: The Daily Routine
Once the ad is live, the phone will start pinging. 
Remember the Golden Rule of WhatsApp leads: **Speed is everything.** If you reply in 5 minutes, they convert. If you reply 3 hours later, they have already gone to another store. 

Have a template ready to copy/paste:
> *"Hi! Yes, the offer is active until May 16th. Are you looking for Silver Articles or Gold Bars? You can visit our Chanda Nagar store anytime between 10 AM to 8 PM to claim this."*
