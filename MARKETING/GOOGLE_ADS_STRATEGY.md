## 💎 Campaign 1: Custom Design Excellence
**Objective:** Capture high-intent customers looking for bespoke, handcrafted jewellery in Hyderabad.

### 1. Targeting
- **Location:** Hyderabad (City + 20km radius targeting Jubilee Hills, Banjara Hills, Gachibowli).
- **Audience:** In-market for "Jewellery," "Engagement Rings," "Wedding Planning."

### 2. Headlines (30 chars max)
1. Design Your Dream Jewellery
2. Custom Gold Jewellery Hyderabad
3. Bespoke Jewelry Artisans
4. Handcrafted Bridal Jewellery
5. Your Design, Our Craftsmanship
6. BIS Hallmarked Custom Gold
7. Artisanal Jewellery Designers

### 3. Descriptions (90 chars max)
1. Turn your imagination into reality. Handcrafted custom jewellery made by master artisans.
2. Direct from artisans in Hyderabad. No middleman, pure BIS Hallmarked gold and diamonds.
3. Bring us your design idea, and we’ll create a masterpiece. Authentic Hyderabadi craft.
4. From engagement rings to bridal sets—get exactly what you want with SMG Jewellers.

### 4. Primary Keywords
- `[custom jewellery hyderabad]`
- `[bespoke jewellery designers hyderabad]`
- `"personalized gold jewellery"`
- `"handmade diamond jewellery makers"`
- `+design +your +own +jewellery +hyderabad`

### 5. Negative Keywords
- `cheap`, `imitation`, `artificial`, `wholesale`, `jobs`, `course`, `how to`, `diy`, `silver plated`, `first copy`.
