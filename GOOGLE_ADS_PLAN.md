# 🎯 Google Ads Campaign Strategy: SMG Jewellers

This is your campaign "Masterplan." As a performance marketing fresher, you can use this structure to set up your first actual campaign in the Google Ads dashboard.

## 1. Campaign Structure (The "Pro" Setup)
We will use a **Single Theme Ad Group (STAG)** structure to keep quality scores high and costs low.

### **Campaign 01: Lead Gen - Custom Jewelry (Hyderabad Search)**
- **Bidding Strategy:** Maximize Conversions (Target CPA once we have 30+ leads).
- **Targeting:** Hyderabad + 15km radius around Chanda Nagar.

#### **Ad Group 1: Pinterest/Instagram Designs**
- **Keywords:** `custom jewelry hyderabad`, `pinterest bridal designs gold`, `customized engagement rings hyderabad`.
- **Logic:** Targeting users who have a visual idea but need a trusted local artisan.

#### **Ad Group 2: Local High-Intent**
- **Keywords:** `best jewelry showroom chanda nagar`, `gold shop near me`, `trusted jewellers hyderabad`.
- **Logic:** Capturing people ready to visit the store immediately.

## 4. Performance Marketer's "Secret Sauce"
- **Negative Keywords:** Add `cheap`, `imitation`, `plastic`, `fake`. You only want people looking for **real gold**.
- **Ad Extensions:** 
  - **Location Extension:** Links to your Google Business Map.
  - **Call Extension:** Shows your phone number directly on the ad.
  - **Sitelink Extensions:** Links to "Our Story," "Gallery," and "Custom Designs."
